Stage 3 · deployment  →  Chapter 5

Optimize, then onboard gradually.

The last step before production is to optimize each service's container — and those optimized containers are what we ship.

per service
optimize Lean, secure, production-ready images.
roll out

verify & repeat
fully deployed Test as you go until the whole system is live.

A gradual, tested rollout — not a big-bang launch. Deployment to production is the focus of Chapter 5.
